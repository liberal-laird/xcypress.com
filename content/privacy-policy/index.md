# Privacy Policy for XCypress

**Last Updated**: 2026-04-10

This Privacy Policy describes how XCypress ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our meditation application ("OMeditation" or the "App"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws and Google Play policies.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent: The OMeditation app does not collect any personal information from users. There are no login requirements, no account creation, and no collection of any personal data. The app functions completely offline without any data transmission to our servers.

### Local Data Only
- Any meditation progress or preferences you set are stored locally on your device only
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
- When you uninstall the app, all data is removed from your device

## How the App Works

- The app functions entirely offline
- No registration or login is required
- No internet connection is needed for core functionality
- All features work without collecting any personal information

## Third-Party Services

While our app does not collect any data, please note that:

- **Analytics**: We do not use any analytics services
- **Cloud Storage**: We do not store any user data on our servers
- **Payment Processing**: Any in-app purchases are processed entirely through platform providers (Apple App Store or Google Play) without any personal information being shared with us

## Data Security

Although no data is collected from users, we maintain robust security practices for any technical data related to the app's operation:
- Secure server configurations
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication for our internal systems

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:
- Update the "Last Updated" date
- Notify you through the App or via email
- Highlight significant changes

Your continued use of the App after the changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
